Fried Chicken Tenders

Wow just look at those crispy tendies!

This fried chicken tenders recipe makes crispy, delicious chicken pieces, paired with our own savory buffalo dipping sauce. We serve it with our famous french fries and buttery texas toast.

Per Serving: 821 calories; protein 45.2g; carbohydrates 35.2g; fat 55.2g; cholesterol 163.8mg; sodium 782.8mg.

Ingredients

Fried Chicken Tenders

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 cups Italian-style seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 24 chicken tenderloins
  • 2 quarts oil for frying

Steps

Fried Chicken Tenders

  1. Prepare the breading station: Place flour in a shallow bowl. Place eggs and water in a second shallow bowl. Place bread crumbs in a third shallow bowl, and mix in ground black pepper and cayenne pepper.
  2. Coat chicken in flour, then eggs, and then bread crumbs, one piece at a time, and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Fry chicken in small batches until the pieces are golden brown, 6 to 8 minutes. Remove chicken and drain on paper towels.
  5. Make the dipping sauce: Combine mayonnaise, sour cream, horseradish, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce in a small bowl. Mix well, and serve with chicken.

Ingredients

Savory Buffalo Dipping Sauce

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 & 1/2 cups hot sauce (like Frank's Hot Sauce)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Steps

Savory Buffalo Dipping Sauce

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat. Once melted remove the saucepan from the heat.
  2. Whisk in the garlic powder, onion powder, honey, and hot sauce until well combined, about 1 minute.
  3. Stir in Worcestershire sauce and white vinegar.
  4. Use immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator up to 2 weeks.
